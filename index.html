Stick Dungeon


This game is sort of what you'd get if you combined a dungeon crawler with a platformer. Send your stickman on an adventure into this dungeon - who knows what you will discover in it's depths?
Controls:
  • Arrow keys to move, up to jump
  • A to use items (that you're holding)
  • S to interact with items in the background (ex: doors)
  • D to view your items
